Who am I?

Hoo boy. Grab a cup of coffee (or something stronger, I'm partial to bourbon myself) and sit a spell. We got a few things to tell here. Wife, Mother, Sagittarian, Dragon, the occasional smarta--, I am all of that and more. Lady Gin Swift, Bard of the Freakshow, at your service. By night, I work for a local Amazon FC. Inbound is my home department, but I'm crosstrained all over the building so I really don't know what I'll be doing till I show up at night. Even then, I may bounce around the building, depending on where I'm needed. During the day, when I'm not catching up on my beauty sleep, taking care of my three kids (my husband totally counts, he said so himself, so that makes 3) and the two cats, I'm learning how to be a front end web developer. I just graduated from Code Louisville's Front End Web Development class, I'll start taking a Javascript class in Jan 2021 Oh and I'm running a FB fan page for the Swedish metal band Avatar (the picture you see here is of their bassist Henrik Sandelin, I snapped this Jan 2018 Louisville KY). Oh and I'm the defacto tech support of the family. Did I mention my Twitch streams? Oh I do that too, when I get the time. I'm the very definition of a filthy casual gamer, but I have my fun.
