Welcome to Sevdawn Photography!

I am NOT a pro photographer. I say all the time that 'I'm no pro photog, just a fan with a smartphone' That said, over the years I've accumulated many incredible pics with just a smartphone, GIMP photo editor, and LOTS of trial and error. 'Crazy Stories' takes you to a page detailing just six of the countless stories I've accumulated over the years. 'Gallery' takes you to a page of just a few examples of my photos over the years. I am just starting on my journey of chronicling my photos as I have quite literally hundreds of photos I've snapped in venues all over the US since 2015.

Spirit of the Clown

I first discovered the Swedish metal band AVATAR in Oct 2014 at the first Louder than Life festival in Louisville KY. May 2015 was my first time dressing up as AVATAR singer Johannes Eckerström (there's a pic of it on the stories page). 'Bard of the Freakshow' is a name I gave myself years ago as I dress up, sing, and tell stories about this amazing band. This pic I snapped Feb 2018 Cleveland OH, the quote is from Captain Spaulding in 'The Devil's Rejects' and now this meme is my work desktop background. It's gotten some great reactions.
