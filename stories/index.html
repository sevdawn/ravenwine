Cosplay Beginnings

May 2015 Louisville KY was my 1st cosplay for a show, dressing as Avatar vocalist Johannes Eckerström. This pic was taken that night after the show. I've since dressed up for almost all of their shows and I've had my pic taken countless times by fans and professional photographers.

Rock on the Range 2016

This is a post from ROTR's FB page, yes that is a pic of me and a pic of AVATAR's vocalist Johannes Eckerström taken that day. People posting pics on there they had taken with me that day and said I wore it better. Um, thanks I guess? LOL

"Raven Wine"

The background of the main page is this one, a pic of Avatar's bassist Henrik Sandelin I snapped during their Jan 2018 Louisville, KY show. 'Raven Wine' is a song from their 6th album "Feathers and Flesh"

Too Cool

Short E Dangerously of Hellzapoppin Circus Sideshow Revue performing in Louisville KY May 2019. Hellzapoppin actually used this pic on their IG to promote future shows and credited me. I might have fangirled a tiny bit.

Matt and El Tigre

Matt DiRito, Pop Evil bassist, with our friend El Tigre. We take El Tigre on many adventures, he has his own Twitter, FB and IG at ElTigreAdventures. El Tigre has pawprints drawn on his feet from Matt and Avatar guitarists Tim Öhrstrom and Jonas Jarlsby.

Ever Watchful

Avatar drummer John Alfredsson performing at the world famous Machine Shop, Flint MI May 2018. I don't know what it is about this little venue in Flint, MI (and I mean TINY, like, 200 person capacity at best) but it's a favorite place for many bands.
